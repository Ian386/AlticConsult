**THE PROBLEMS WE SOLVE**

Operating a business in Kenya requires navigating a complex regulatory and tax environment.

Many businesses face challenges such as:

**Complex Tax Compliance:** Kenya’s tax laws and filing requirements can expose businesses to penalties and regulatory risks if not managed correctly.

**KRA Audits and Tax Disputes:** Unexpected tax assessments and audits can disrupt operations and create financial uncertainty.

**Weak Financial Management Systems:** Inadequate accounting systems limit financial visibility and hinder effective decision-making.

**Regulatory and Statutory Compliance:** Businesses must comply with various regulatory requirements including tax registration, payroll compliance and statutory reporting.

**Limited Financial Insight for Growth:** Many businesses lack the financial clarity required to make strategic decisions and scale effectively.

Altic Consult works with clients to **resolve these challenges through structured advisory, strong compliance frameworks and strategic financial guidance.**
